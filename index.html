About Lion Windows and Doors

Founded in 2008, Lion Windows and Doors has been proudly serving the Greater Toronto Area with exceptional window and door solutions. Our mission is simple: to provide superior products, expert installation, and outstanding customer service.

We understand that your windows and doors are more than just functional elements - they're integral to your home's comfort, security, and aesthetic appeal. That's why we go above and beyond to ensure every project meets our exacting standards.
